About Our Chapter

The IEEE Industry Applications Society (IAS) Student Branch Chapter at Daffodil International University is dedicated to the advancement of the theory and practice of electrical and electronic engineering in the development, design, manufacture, and application of electrical systems. We bridge the gap between academia and industry.
